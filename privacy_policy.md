# Privacy Policy for Smart Reply AI

**Last Updated:** May 26, 2026

Avnish Mavi ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Smart Reply AI mobile application ("the App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

Smart Reply AI requires specific device permissions to function effectively:

*   **Notification Access:** The App requires the "Notification Access" permission to read incoming messages locally on your device. This allows the App to generate smart, contextual replies. **Your notification data is never stored on our servers or shared with any third party other than the AI provider (Google Gemini) for the explicit purpose of generating replies.**
*   **Display Over Other Apps:** This permission is used to display the smart reply interface over your current screen so you can quickly copy the suggested responses.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, process data, or serve advertisements:

*   **Google Gemini API:** The text from your notifications is sent securely directly from your device to the Google Gemini API to generate the AI responses.
*   **Google AdMob:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads.
*   **Firebase Analytics & Remote Config:** We use Firebase to understand how users interact with our App and to manage App configurations remotely, helping us improve the user experience.

These third-party services have their own Privacy Policies addressing how they use such information:
*   [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 3. Data Storage and Security

*   **No Centralized Storage:** We do not operate custom backend servers to store your personal messages or notifications. All processing is done either locally on your device or via the direct connection to Google's Gemini API.
*   **User Control:** The App operates on a "no auto-send" policy. The user must manually review and copy the generated replies. We do not automatically send messages on your behalf.
*   **Security:** We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 4. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the developer contact email provided on our Google Play Store listing.
